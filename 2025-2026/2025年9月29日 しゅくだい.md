**Basic info**
• Romaji: shirarete imasu  
• Hiragana: しられています  
• Kanji: 知られています  
• Word type: Verb phrase (passive + polite progressive)
• Base verb: 知る (shiru = to know)
• Verb group: Godan (u-verb)

**Meaning**
• “Is known” / “is well-known” / “is recognized”  
• Passive form of 知る → 知られる = “to be known”  
• 知られています = polite present/progressive “is known”

**Grammar breakdown**
• 知る (shiru) = to know  
• Passive: 知られる (shiraru → shirareru) = to be known  
• Polite/progressive: 知られています = is (being) known

**Examples**
• メイさんはトップギアでも知られています。  
  *Mei-san wa Toppugia demo shirarete imasu.*  
  “Mr. May is also known from Top Gear.”

• この町は温泉で知られています。  
  *Kono machi wa onsen de shirarete imasu.*  
  “This town is known for its hot springs.”

• 富士山は日本で一番高い山として知られています。  
  *Fujisan wa Nihon de ichiban takai yama to shite shirarete imasu.*  
  “Mount Fuji is known as the highest mountain in Japan.”
