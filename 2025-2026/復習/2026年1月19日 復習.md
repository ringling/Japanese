## 文法 (Grammar Points)

### 1. Vています (ongoing state/action)
**Formation**: Verb て-form + います
**Usage**: Describes current state or ongoing action
- めがねをかけています = is wearing glasses
- わらっています = is laughing/smiling  
- ないています = is crying
- たっています = is standing
- すわっています = is sitting

### 2. Vている人 (person who is doing...)
**Formation**: Verb て-form + いる + 人
**Usage**: To identify people by their current state or action
- めがねをかけている人 = person who is wearing glasses
- たっている人 = person who is standing
- ないている人 = person who is crying

### 3. どの人ですか (which person?)
- ともだちは どの人ですか = Which person is your friend?
- **Usage**: To identify specific individuals from a group

### 4. Physical description patterns
- せが高いです = is tall
- かみがながいです = has long hair  
- **Pattern**: Body part + が + adjective + です

### 5. どんな人ですか (what kind of person?)
**Usage**: Asking about someone's personality or characteristics
- Answers can include personality adjectives or physical descriptions
