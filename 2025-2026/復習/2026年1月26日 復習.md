## 文法 (Grammar Points)

### 1. どの人ですか (Which person is it?)
**Usage**: To identify specific individuals in a group
- ともだちはどの人ですか = Which person is your friend?

### 2. Vている人 (Person who is doing...)
**Formation**: Verb て-form + いる + 人
- めがねをかけている人 = person wearing glasses
- 立っている人 = person who is standing

### 3. どんな人ですか (What kind of person?)
**Usage**: Asking about personality or characteristics
- Answers with personality adjectives or physical descriptions
