## 文法 (Grammar Points)

### ～そう (Looks like/Seems like)
- **Formation**: 
  - i-adjectives: Remove い + そう (おいしい → おいしそう)
  - na-adjectives: Add そう (やさしな → やさしそう)
- **Usage**: Expresses appearance or what something seems like
- **Examples**:
  - **やさしそうなおくさんですね** - Your wife looks kind
  - **おいしそうにケーキを食べています** - Eating cake in a way that looks delicious

### ～と いっしょに (Together with)
- **Formation**: Noun + と + いっしょに
- **Usage**: Expresses doing something together with someone/something
- **Example**: **家族といっしょに来ました** - Came together with family

### ～んですよ (Explanatory ending)
- **Usage**: Gives explanation or emphasis to a statement
- **Example**: **ときどききびしいんですよ** - Sometimes she's strict, you know
