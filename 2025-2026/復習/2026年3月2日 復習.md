## 文法 (Grammar Points)

### を やります (Will do/study)
- **Structure**: Object + を + やります
- **Example**: **だい3かを やります** - We will do Lesson 3
- **Usage**: For studying/doing lessons, homework, activities

### から (from) with pages/lessons
- **Structure**: Starting point + から + ending point
- **Example**: **35ページから39ページ** - From page 35 to page 39
- **Usage**: Indicates range or starting point

### Lesson structure vocabulary
- **だい～か** (第～課) - Lesson ~ (counter for lessons)
- **りかい** (理解) - Understanding/comprehension
- **かつどう** (活動) - Activities
