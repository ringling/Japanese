#### To much 

**Basic info**
• Romaji: misugimashita
• Hiragana: みすぎました
• Kanji: 見すぎました
• Word type: Verb phrase (見る = to see + すぎる = to do too much)

**Structure**
• 見る (miru, to see/look/watch) + すぎる (sugiru, to overdo)  
→ 見すぎる (misugiru) = to watch too much  
→ Polite past: 見すぎました (misugimashita) = watched too much

**Meaning**
• Watched too much  
• Looked too much  
• Overdid watching/seeing

**Forms**
• Dictionary: 見すぎる (misugiru) = to watch too much  
• Negative: 見すぎない (misuginai) = don’t watch too much  
• Past plain: 見すぎた (misugita) = watched too much  
• Past negative: 見すぎなかった (misuginakatta) = didn’t watch too much  
• Polite present: 見すぎます (misugimasu) = watch too much  
• Polite negative: 見すぎません (misugimasen) = don’t watch too much  
• Polite past: 見すぎました (misugimashita) = watched too much  
• Polite past negative: 見すぎませんでした (misugimasen deshita) = didn’t watch too much  

**Examples**
• テレビを見すぎました。  
  *Terebi o misugimashita.*  
  “I watched too much TV.”

• コンピューターを見すぎないでください。  
  *Konpyūtā o misuginaide kudasai.*  
  “Please don’t look at the computer too much.”
