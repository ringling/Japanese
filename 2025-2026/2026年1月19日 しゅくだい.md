Here’s a **clear explanation of the page**, with the **key grammar** and **all numbered phrases** translated into **English**, plus **hiragana + romaji** so you can study it cleanly.

---

## **Title**

  

**友だちは どの 人ですか**

- **Hiragana:** ともだちは どの ひとですか
    
- **Romaji:** tomodachi wa dono hito desu ka
    
- **English:** _Which person is your friend?_
    

---

## **Grammar note (top-right)**

  

**話すとき**

- **Vています = Vています**
    
    → “is doing …” (action happening now / current state)
    
- **Vている人 = Vている人**
    
    → “a person who is doing …”
    

  

This page practices **describing people by what they are doing or wearing**.

## **Adjectives (section ②)**

  

**どんな 人ですか**

_What kind of person is (he/she)?_

- **かわいい** – cute
    
- **まじめ** – serious
    
- **きびしい** – strict
    
- **頭がいい** – smart
    
- **かっこいい** – cool
    
- **元気** – energetic
    
- **おもしろい** – interesting / funny
    
- **やさしい** – kind
    
- **きれい** – beautiful / clean
    

---

## **Core pattern you’re meant to use**

  

> **Vている人です。**

> _They are a person who is …_

  

Example:

- **めがねを かけている 人です。**
    
    _A person who is wearing glasses._
