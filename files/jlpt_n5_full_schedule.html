How the three tracks fit together

Kanji drives the weekly theme. Whatever kanji you're learning that week, the vocabulary and grammar track follow the same theme — so when Week 2 introduces time/calendar kanji, you're also drilling time-related vocab and the grammar patterns for telling time.

Grammar Tuesday targets the gap-check items first (〜たい, 〜ができます, 〜になる, counters), then moves into N4 patterns alongside Marugoto. Most N5 grammar is already solid — you're maintaining and filling gaps, not starting from scratch.

Vocabulary is interleaved into kanji days as 5-10 min add-ons, then consolidated on Saturday. ~50 new words/week = ~400 over 8 weeks, which closes the N5 vocab gap.
